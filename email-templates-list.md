# CIM Amplify Email Templates - Complete List

This document lists ALL email templates and their content that our system sends to users.

## 1. Welcome Emails

### Welcome Email for Buyers
**Subject:** `Welcome to CIM Amplify!`
**Recipient:** New buyer registrations
**Content:**
```
Hello [Buyer Name],

Welcome to CIM Amplify! We're excited to have you join our platform.

As a buyer on CIM Amplify, you'll receive these benefits:
• M&A Advisors personally invite you to deals that match your criteria
• No scrolling — Directly receive deals in your inbox matched to your criteria
• Exclusive deals because we reward Advisors who post exclusively with us
• 0.5% (50 basis points) buyer fee. By far the lowest in the industry
• We get out of the way – no requirement to communicate through the platform
• All deals at least $1 Million in EBITDA or $5 Million in revenue

Please to make sure you complete your investment profile so we can match you with the best opportunities.

[Go to Buyer Dashboard Button]

If you have any questions, feel free to reply to this email or visit our FAQ section.
We look forward to helping you with your deal-making journey!

Thank you,
The CIM Amplify Team
```

## 6. LOI (Letter of Intent) Emails

### Deal Paused for LOI - Advisor Notification
**Subject:** `Your Deal Has Been Paused for LOI`
**Recipient:** Advisors when their deal is paused for LOI
**Content:**
```
Hello [Advisor Name],

Your deal [Deal Title] has been paused for Letter of Intent (LOI) negotiations.

While your deal is paused, it will not be visible to new buyers on the marketplace. Existing active buyers have been notified about this status change.

When you are ready to make the deal active again, you can revive it from your LOI Deals dashboard. If the deal does sell please click Off Market and let us know the details of the sale.

[View LOI Deals Button]

Thank you,
The CIM Amplify Team
```

### Deal Paused for LOI - Active Buyer Notification
**Subject:** `Deal Update: [Deal Title] - Paused for LOI`
**Recipient:** Active buyers when a deal they're interested in is paused for LOI

[Browse Marketplace Button]

The CIM Amplify Team

## Email Template Structure

All emails use a consistent HTML template structure with:
- CIM Amplify branding and logo
- Responsive design for mobile compatibility
- Consistent color scheme (teal/navy theme)
- Standard footer with unsubscribe information
- Inline CSS for email client compatibility

## Technical Notes

- All emails are sent via Gmail SMTP using nodemailer
- Email delivery is logged in the communication_logs collection
- Failed emails are queued for retry in the email_queue collection
- All emails include the CIM Amplify illustration as an attachment
- Email content uses the `genericEmailTemplate` wrapper for consistent formatting
- Monthly reports use rich HTML templates with actual deal data and buyer information
- LOI functionality includes comprehensive email notifications for all stakeholders
- Deal closing includes different email flows for CIM Amplify sales vs. external sales
